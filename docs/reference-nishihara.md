## Useful Facts

- Facility: 西原町民体育館
- Office phone: 098-945-8095
- Office fax: 098-945-8096
- Address: 〒903-0117 西原町字翁長956番地
- Operating hours: 8:30-21:30
- Reservation window: accepted from the 1st day of the previous month for the intended use month
- Formal use application: submit at the gym office by 10 days before use with representative seal and fee ticket
- Uses listed: badminton, basketball, volleyball, judo hall, kendo hall, meeting room
- System note: the linked public facility reservation system cannot complete reservations, and availability is not real time

## Fees Noted On Source Page

Hourly base fees shown on the source page include:

- Full arena: town resident 2,800 yen / outside town 5,600 yen
- Badminton court: town resident 500 yen / outside town 1,000 yen
- Volleyball court: town resident 1,400 yen / outside town 2,800 yen
- Basketball court: town resident 500 yen / outside town 1,000 yen
- Judo/kendo hall: town resident 500 yen / outside town 700 yen
- Meeting room AC: 700 yen
- Shower: 100 yen per person per use

## Planning Implications

- Call the office before assuming dates are available.
- Prepare paperwork and payment before the 10-day-before-use deadline.
- If this becomes a member event, decide whether the activity is amateur sports or another use category before budgeting.
